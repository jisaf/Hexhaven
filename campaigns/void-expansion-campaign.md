# The Void Expansion Campaign

## Campaign Overview
**Theme**: Sci-Fi - Space Stations and Alien Planets
**Setting**: Humanity spreads across the stars aboard colony ships and space stations. But something is spreading through the colonies - an ancient alien intelligence that consumes worlds. Players must defend the frontier and discover the source.
**Difficulty Progression**: 1 → 7
**Total Scenarios**: 15
**Player Count**: 1-4

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Colony Ship Breach
**Difficulty**: 1
**Status**: Starting Scenario
**Map Size**: 76 hexes
**Background**: Interior corridors of a massive colony ship. Metal walls with glowing panels. Emergency lights cast red shadows. Viewport shows distant stars and an ominous approaching shadow.

**Description**:
Deep in space, your colony ship detects a catastrophic breach. Unknown aliens have infiltrated the vessel. You must reach the command center and seal the sections before the ship is lost.

**Monsters**: Alien Drone (3), Alien Scout (2)
**Treasures**: Repair kit (restores ship systems), Emergency beacon
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Reach the command center and seal the breach
- Secondary: Protect the evacuation pods (prevent more than 1 from being destroyed)

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: Alarms blare - "Unknown hostiles aboard! All hands to combat stations!"
- **Corridor Breach**: Alien creatures emerge from ventilation shafts
- **Halfway Point**: "They're spreading through the ship! Seal the doors!"
- **Victory**: Emergency bulkheads slam shut; breach is contained

**Unlocks**: Scenario 2 (Station Alpha Defense)

### Scenario 2: Station Alpha Defense
**Difficulty**: 1
**Map Size**: 80 hexes
**Background**: A cylindrical space station. Docking bays, observation decks, and residential modules. Vacuum visible through reinforced windows. Explosions flicker across exterior.

**Description**:
The colony ship reaches the nearest station and broadcasts a warning. Station Alpha is under attack by the same aliens. You must defend critical infrastructure as evacuation shuttles escape.

**Monsters**: Alien Drone (4), Alien Swarm (2)
**Treasures**: Shield generator override code, Station command logs
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Defend the evacuation bay for 4 rounds
- Secondary: Activate the backup power (restore station shields)

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "We've got incoming! Alien signatures all over the station!"
- **Round 1**: First drones breach the bay
- **Round 2**: "Shields at 30%! We're losing them!"
- **Power Restored**: "Shields back online!"
- **Round 4**: Final shuttle launches
- **Victory**: Station is secured; survivors evacuate to a safe location

**Unlocks**: Scenario 4 (Mining Station Overrun)
**Also Available**: Scenario 3 (Alien Wreckage Salvage - side quest)

### Scenario 3: Alien Wreckage Salvage
**Difficulty**: 1
**Map Size**: 72 hexes
**Background**: A graveyard of wrecked spacecraft and space debris. Floating wreckage creates a maze. Sparks and venting atmosphere. Strange hieroglyphic symbols on the alien vessels.

**Description**:
Among the debris orbiting Station Alpha is an alien vessel, severely damaged but intact. Scientists believe it holds clues about the alien threat. Salvage teams must board and retrieve data.

**Monsters**: Alien Scavenger (3), Alien Tech Unit (1), Alien Swarm (1)
**Treasures**: Alien data core (crucial intel), Energy cells
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Retrieve the alien data core from the wreck's center
- Secondary: Document 3 alien hieroglyphs before evacuating

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "Careful - this ship is alien. We don't know what's still operational."
- **Data Core Located**: "We've found something! The patterns... they're mathematical!"
- **Glyph 1 Found**: Ancient writing tells of an empire that consumed worlds
- **Glyph 2 Found**: References to a homeworld at the edge of known space
- **Glyph 3 Found**: A warning: "They return to consume all"
- **Victory**: Data suggests the aliens are from a dying civilization seeking conquest

**Unlocks**: None (side scenario)

### Scenario 4: Mining Station Overrun
**Difficulty**: 2
**Map Size**: 94 hexes
**Background**: An industrial mining complex on an asteroid. Drilling equipment, ore processors, storage silos. One section is infested with alien growth.

**Description**:
The remote mining outpost hasn't transmitted in 48 hours. A rescue team finds it overrun. The aliens have taken over mineral-rich tunnels and are converting resources. Reclaim or destroy the facility.

**Monsters**: Alien Drone (3), Alien Warrior (2), Xenomorph (1)
**Treasures**: Rare mineral core, Mining colony database
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Destroy the infestation growth (eliminate Xenomorph)
- Secondary: Recover mining logs without triggering a meltdown

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "This place is a graveyard. They've taken everyone."
- **Growth Found**: Organic biomass covers the walls - it's spreading
- **Xenomorph Revealed**: A larger, more intelligent alien emerges
- **Xenomorph Defeated**: "We've killed the colony mother!"
- **Victory**: Mining operation is lost, but you've gained valuable data on the aliens

**Unlocks**: Scenario 5 & 6 (choose path, or complete both)

- **Station Reached**: "Where is everyone? Oh no... oh no..."
- **Researchers Found**: "The creatures came from the sky. They changed everything."

- **Victory**: The planet is quarantined; its biosphere cannot be saved

**Unlocks**: Scenario 7 (Research Station Lockdown)

### Scenario 7: Research Station Lockdown
**Difficulty**: 3
**Map Size**: 96 hexes
**Background**: A massive orbital research station. Multiple modules, labs, docking rings. Atmosphere processors hum. Observatory dome looks out at the stars. Lockdown barriers seal corridors.

**Description**:
The research station detects anomalous signals throughout the sector. Protocols activate automatically, sealing the station. You must navigate the emergency lockdown, investigate the source, and decide whether this station can be saved.

**Monsters**: Alien Infiltrator (2), Alien Engineer (2), Military Robot (1)
**Treasures**: Research archives (contains alien origin data), Override codes
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Reach the main lab and access the research (understand the alien origin)
- Secondary**: Prevent the self-destruct from initiating (unless necessary)

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "Lockdown sequence activated. All personnel to designated stations."
- **Infiltrators Found**: Aliens have walked among us, disguised in research areas
- **Lab Reached**: "My god... they found it. They found the proof."
- **Research Complete**: The aliens are from another universe, leaking through dimensions
- **Victory**: You now understand the true nature of the threat

**Unlocks**: Scenario 8 & 9 (choose path, or complete both)

### Scenario 13: The Wormhole Transit

**Description**:
You enter the wormhole itself - the dimensional gateway the aliens use to invade from their universe. The journey is disorienting and dangerous. Your team must reach the alien universe's gateway station before the wormhole destabilizes.

**Monsters**: Void Entity (2), Dimensional Wraith (2), Wormhole Guard (1)
**Treasures**: Dimensional key (opens barriers), Reality anchor
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Reach the far side of the wormhole
- Secondary**: Survive dimensional turbulence without taking critical damage

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "We're entering another universe. Hold steady!"
- **Turbulence**: "Space is tearing apart around us!"
- **Halfway Point**: You see the alien universe beyond - a landscape of impossible geometry
- **Entities Encountered**: Creatures native to the void between universes attack
- **Exit Visible**: "We're almost through!"
- **Victory**: You emerge in the alien universe - at their main staging area

### Scenario 14: The Infected Sector
**Difficulty**: 6
**Map Size**: 104 hexes
**Background**: A war-torn sector of the alien universe. Entire space stations destroyed. Fleets of biological warships dock at orbital stations. The infrastructure of invasion visible everywhere. This is their civilization - vast, ancient, and utterly alien.

**Description**:
You've reached the heart of the alien invasion effort. This sector is their primary invasion staging area. Countless ship-yards build vessels for the assault. Millions of aliens coordinate their strategy here. Crippling this sector will halt their advance for months.

**Monsters**: Alien General (1 - boss), Alien War Caste (3), Alien Elite Guard (2), Alien Swarm (2)
**Treasures**: Master invasion plans (reveals everything), Biological inhibitor (key to final scenario)
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Defeat the Alien General commanding this sector
- Secondary**: Destroy at least 3 of the 5 ship-building facilities

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: "This is their world. We're so far from home..."
- **General Appears**: A towering, ancient alien of incredible presence
- **Combat Begins**: His warriors fight with perfect coordination
- **Facilities Destroyed**: "Their infrastructure crumbles!"
- **General 50% Health**: "You die now. Your species ends here."
- **General Defeated**: "IMPOSSIBLE! Our collective consciousness... BROKEN!"
- **Victory**: Invasion command is shattered; the war effort delays

**Unlocks**: Scenario 15 (The Origin Nexus - FINAL)

### Scenario 15: The Origin Nexus
**Difficulty**: 7 (FINAL)
**Map Size**: 112 hexes
**Background**: The highest tier alien fortress-temple. Biological and technological hybrid architecture. Pulsing with dimensional energy. At its center: a massive collective consciousness node - the hive mind made physical. Stars visible through transparent walls reveal we're at the sector's heart.

**Description**:
You've reached the Origin Nexus - the consciousness core of the entire alien collective. This is where all decisions originate. Thousands of aliens are physically merged with the structure. Destroying this will kill the collective consciousness across light-years of space.

**Monsters**: The Architect Prime (2-health boss), Consciousness Fragment (3), Reality Guard (2)
**Treasures**: Dimensional codex (ultimate knowledge), Collective memory cores
**Objectives**:
- Primary: Destroy the Consciousness Core (requires penetrating inner sanctum)
- Secondary**: Free the consciousness nodes without destroying them (contains knowledge)

**Narrative Triggers**:
- **Intro**: You stand in the presence of something that has consumed trillions.
- **Architect Manifests**: "I am the Architect. I have conquered universes. What are you?"
- **Combat Begins**: The Architect is one with the station - all its weapons are his
- **Architect 50% Health**: "Your defiance is... fascinating. I will savor your extinction."
- **Core Penetrated**: Barriers fall; you approach the consciousness itself
- **Consciousness Freed**: "What have you done? We are... ourselves again?"
- **Architect Defeated**: A scream of agony as the consciousness fractures
- **Core Destroyed**: The dimensional gateway collapses
- **Victory**: The entire invasion is reversed - aliens are pulled back to their universe

**Campaign End**:
- **Victory Narrative**: Humanity survives. The wormholes seal. Life returns to the infected worlds slowly.
- **Defeat Narrative**: The universe falls to the collective. Resistance becomes impossible.

## Campaign Themes & Progression

**Act 1 (Scenarios 1-4): The Threat Emerges**
- First contact with the aliens
- Realize they're spreading through the colonies
- Begin understanding their nature

**Act 2 (Scenarios 5-9): Understanding**
- Investigate their origins
- Learn they're from another dimension
- Discover they're opening wormholes deliberately

**Act 3 (Scenarios 10-12): Counteroffensive**
- Attack their hive world
- Infiltrate their command structure
- Prepare to enter their dimension

**Act 4 (Scenarios 13-15): Invasion**
- Cross into the alien universe
- Strike at their staging area
- Confront the Architect Prime and end the threat
